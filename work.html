Stories Beneath the Shell

Over the course of two semester, Spring 2019 and Fall 2019, I was a staff writer for an online campus publication, "Stories Beneath the Shell." Each week, I was responsible for pitching story ideas to editors and writing one weekly story. I wrote news, features and multimedia stories. After a year of writing, I was promoted to my current position, Assistant General Assignment Editor. Every week, I hold a general assignment meeting that is open to students of all majors, I create the general assignment news budget and I edit all of our general assignment articles.
