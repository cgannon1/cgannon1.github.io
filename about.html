Casey Gannon

About

Casey Gannon is an undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park pursuing a bachelor of art's degree in multiplatform journalism.

Gannon's interest in journalism sparked when she joined her high school newspaper. She eventually became editor-in-chief her senior year, and she decided to apply to journalism programs for college. Gannon choose to continue her journalism education at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism because of their rigorous curriculum, outstanding staff and close proximetity to Washington, D.C.
